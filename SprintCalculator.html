Frank Dick Chart Interface - Predict Performance Based On Sprint Times:
This calculator is based on the chart for sprint performance in the article
'Development of Maximum Sprinting Speed' by Frank W. Dick.

Enter data in any of the input boxes and click calculate to display the results.
The lower and upper bounds for the input data will be listed:
30m from blocks (3.58-5.50), 30m flying (2.48-4.40), 60m from blocks (HT) (6.22-8.50), 150m standing (HT) (14.87-20.80),
250m standing (HT) (25.47-35.60), 60m electronic (6.49-8.90), 100m electronic (10.09-13.90), 200m electronic (20.17-28.00).
